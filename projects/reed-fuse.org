** Long term current capability (LTC)

Procedure:
- test the reed switch
- turn on current
- wait Time
- turn off current
- test the reed switch

** Flash current capability (FCC)

Procedure:
1. test the reed switch
2. turn on current
3. wait Time or until circuit breaks (which is the expected result)
4. turn off current
5. test the reed switch
