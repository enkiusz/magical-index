** Long term current capability (LTC)

Procedure:
1. test the reed switch
1. turn on current
1. wait Time
1. turn off current
1. test the reed switch

** Flash current capability (FCC)

Procedure:
1. test the reed switch
1. turn on current
1. wait Time or until circuit breaks (which is the expected result)
1. turn off current
1. test the reed switch
